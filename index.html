About Me

I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at New York University's Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, where I'm advised by Benjamin Peherstorfer. I specialize in generative modeling with a focus on:

  • Physics-based simulations
  • Video synthesis and generation techniques

My research bridges the gap between advanced generative modeling techniques and their applications in video synthesis and PDE-based simulations. I've been recognized with the Harold Grad Memorial Prize and received the Maccracken Fellowship for my work.
