Welcome!
I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at NYU, Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences. I am advised by Benjamin Peherstorfer. I am currently focused on developing methods numerical methods for nonlinear model reduction and high dimensional PDEs using neural networks and other machine learning techniques.

Previously I was a researcher at Flatiron Institute in New York City where I worked under Dmitri Chklovskii in the Neural Circuts and Algorithms group within the Center for Computational Neuroscience.

Previous to graduate studies and scientific research, I was a Software Engineer for 3 years at Bloomberg LP.
